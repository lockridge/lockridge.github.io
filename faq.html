How old do I need to be to go bow fishing?

Old enough to draw a 20lbs bow back.

Do I need my own equipment?

NO! We provide all equipment needed.

Do I need a hunting or fishing license?

YES you will need to have a valid fishing license.

Where do I need to go the day of the trip?

You will receive the boat ramp address after booking your trip and the text message the day of the trip.

How long is a trip?

6-hour minimum trip may run longer depending on fishing conditions.

Will I be standing the entire trip?

The only time spent standing is while fishing. The boat has plenty of sitting room if breaks need to be taken.

What do we do with the fish?

Most of the fish we will be shooting are considered trash fish but customers are more than welcome to keep any of the fish we shoot. If not we will dispose of the fish properly.

How many fish can we shoot?

When bow fishing we target invasive species so lucky for us there is no limit we can shoot until are arms get sore. There are a few species that do have a limit so your guide will inform you before your trip gets started.

How many people can go on a trip?

4 shooters and 2 ride along
