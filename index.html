All Angles Outfitters

We provide bowfishing and outdoor experiences.

All Angles Outfitters is a veteran owned and operated full service bow fishing outfitter. Our guides are experienced outdoorsman with fun and friendly attitudes that want you to have a bow fishing trip you will never forget. Our goal is to provide you with an outdoor experience that will keep you wanting to come back for more.

Kentucky water ways hold a wide range of species that you will be allowed to harvest depending on which one of our beautiful lakes you decide to fish. Our trips are a minimum of 6 hours all equipment and safety gear is provided as well as snacks and drinks. Although ever trip is unique and different in its own way we guarantee will do our very best as guides to put you on as many fish as possible.
